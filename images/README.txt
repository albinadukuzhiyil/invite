PICTURES FOLDER
---------------
Replace photo1.png ... photo6.png with your own wedding photos.

Easiest way: name your photos exactly photo1.png, photo2.png, etc.
(or .jpg) and drop them in here. If you change the names or the
number of photos, update the PICTURES list near the top of
index.html (inside the <script>, in the CONFIG/PICTURES area).

Portrait images (taller than wide, around 600x800 or larger) look
best in the moving gallery.
